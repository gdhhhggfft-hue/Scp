Origin

No one knows where he came from

The Eomesk is a strange and mysterious entity from Boundaryless Liminal Expanse. No one truly knows where he came from, how old he is, or whether he was ever created at all. Many stories contradict one another, and every attempt to fully understand him only seems to create more questions.

Identifying Features

Blue. Rubber. Smiling.

He is known for his blue, rubber-like body, white eyes, and a large smile that never disappears. Yet that smile is not peaceful, friendly, or reassuring. It carries an unusual feeling of confidence, mockery, amusement, and challenge — as though The Eomesk is always aware of something nobody else understands.

Physical Behavior — Logged Anomaly 01

His body does not behave like a normal body

It bends, stretches, twists, flattens, melts, inflates, folds, and reforms in impossible ways. Physical rules appear inconsistent around him. Sometimes attacks pass through him. Sometimes his body absorbs force. Sometimes it stretches far beyond what should be possible before snapping back with astonishing speed.

When struck, parts of his body may suddenly pull backward as though attached to an invisible force. A loud stretching or pulling sound follows before the rubber snaps forward, launching a counterattack from an unexpected direction. Opponents often find themselves unable to predict how his body will react.

Movement Pattern

He moves like nothing else does

The Eomesk's movements are equally unusual. He may suddenly transform into a bouncing sphere, roll across impossible surfaces, launch himself through the air, or move in ways that seem almost artificial. His jumps, flights, landings, and rapid movements are often accompanied by strange cartoon-like sounds. However, these sounds never make him appear weak or childish. Instead, they create an unsettling contrast with his overwhelming presence.

Inventory Anomaly — Logged Anomaly 02

Something is behind his back

Many believe that The Eomesk possesses an endless space hidden behind his back. By simply reaching behind himself, he can retrieve objects of almost any kind. Weapons, machines, vehicles, structures, tools, mysterious artifacts, and objects that seem impossible to identify have all reportedly appeared from this unknown space.

Some stories claim that the objects he retrieves are not created at all. Instead, they may come from forgotten places, abandoned possibilities, lost worlds, or locations beyond ordinary existence. Whether these stories are true remains unknown.

Self-Modification

Detachment without harm

The Eomesk can separate parts of his body without harm. His head, arms, hands, legs, and other pieces can detach and continue functioning independently before reconnecting moments later. No blood appears. No injury remains. The process seems completely natural to him.

Transformation

Becoming something else entirely

Transformation is another aspect of his nature. He can become paper, liquid, machinery, objects, symbols, shapes, and forms that do not appear to follow any known category. At times, witnesses disagree entirely on what they saw him become.

Behavioral Assessment

Not evil. Just unbound.

Despite his immense power, The Eomesk is not considered evil. He rarely acts out of cruelty and does not seek destruction for its own sake. Instead, his actions often appear motivated by amusement, curiosity, provocation, and a desire to challenge expectations.

Audio Anomaly — Logged Anomaly 03

The laugh

His laughter is perhaps his most recognizable trait. It is not joyful laughter, nor is it completely hostile. It carries a strange confidence that seems tied to his very existence. Some believe his laughter is more than a sound. It may be a force, a phenomenon, or even a part of his power itself.

Recurring Theme

Limits seem meaningless around him

Across countless stories, records, and rumors, one theme appears repeatedly: limits seem meaningless around The Eomesk. Rules bend. Expectations fail. Boundaries become uncertain. Even concepts that should remain fixed sometimes behave differently in his presence.

For this reason, many inhabitants of Boundaryless Liminal Expanse do not view The Eomesk as merely a powerful being. They view him as a phenomenon, a mystery, and perhaps even a living contradiction.
